How does RAG work? (In simple terms)

Imagine you're a doctor. Before diagnosing a new patient, you check their previous medical records and think about similar cases you've seen before. That's exactly what RAG does for Sensei AI:

  1. You speak or type your symptoms
  2. RAG searches through a database of clinical knowledge (from AI4Bharat) and past patient sessions
  3. It finds the most similar medical cases using AI-powered semantic matching
  4. This relevant context is injected into the AI's prompt — so it "remembers" similar cases
  5. The AI gives a more accurate, grounded response because it has reference data

📚 Clinical knowledge base sourced from AI4Bharat — covering 20+ Indian clinical conditions in English & Hindi (dengue, TB, malaria, diabetes, hypertension, and more).
